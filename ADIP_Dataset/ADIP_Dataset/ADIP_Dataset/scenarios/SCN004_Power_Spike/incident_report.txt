Incident ID: INC006

Asset: AST004 - Solar Inverter INV01
Customer: CUS002 - Green Solar Ltd

Incident Summary:
A sudden power spike caused the solar inverter to shut down unexpectedly.

Root Cause:
Grid voltage fluctuation and surge protection failure.

Severity:
High

Downtime:
3 hours

Energy Loss:
500 kWh

Resolution:
Surge protection device replaced and inverter restarted successfully.
